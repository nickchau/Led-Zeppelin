Who are Led Zeppelin?

For than 40 years Led Zeppelin have continued to inspire generations with groundbreaking blues infused, guitar driven rock 'n' roll. During their 12 year reign they were the biggest rock band in the world, and remian one of the most influential and innovative groups in music history.

With well over 200 million records sold worldwide, their catelogue is one of the most enduring bodies of musical composition to come out of the 20th century, and it has influenced countless bands along the way. They had the biggest tours; the biggest sound; the biggst record sales; the biggest reputation. Quite Simply Led Zeppelin was the ultimate rock band.

Robert Plant

Lead vocalist and primary lyricist for zeppelin, Plant was the front man for the group. Using his wide vocal range and ability to project to bring his lyrics to life.

Jimmy Page

The founding member of Zeppelin Jimmy brought his fast heavy riffs to the hard rock scene and is often considered one of the greatest rock guitarists of all time.

John Bonham

As the powerhouse of the band, Bonham used impecable technique and ability to hit the skins harder and faster than anyone to create Zeppelins signiture sound.

John-Paul Jones

John-Paul brought an irreplacable prowess at the bass and other instruments to create one of the most vibrant rythem sections in rock music history.
